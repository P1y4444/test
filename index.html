Mechatronics & Robotics

Create the future with advanced automation and robotics technologies

About

This is a completely new specialty that appeared in the secondary education system in 2017, and it did not appear by chance, as mechatronics and robotics are rapidly developing sciences that recently secured their prestigious place in the modern world. Their main goal is to create and operate machines that move and function based on electronic control systems.

Mechatronics and robotics are used in machine tool manufacturing and equipment creation that automates production lines; in military, aerospace, and space technology; in medical, office, and household appliances; in robotics; and in automotive engineering.

Mechatronics

A field dedicated to the study of combined computer and electrical components, which form the basis for designing and creating innovative systems and machines.

Robotics

An area focused on the development and design of robots and systems capable of replacing human labor and automating complex technological processes.

Application areas

Industry

Automation of production lines, machine tool manufacturing, conveyor systems

Aerospace industry

Control systems for aircraft, space robots, drones

Medicine

Surgical robots, prosthetics, diagnostic equipment

Automotive industry

Driver assistance systems, autopilots, electric vehicles

Household appliances

Smart homes, robotic vacuum cleaners, automated systems

Military equipment

Unmanned vehicles, guidance systems, robotic complexes

Training program

At the training stage, future high-level specialists are educated, ready to work with technical systems, units, and complexes that are used to perform specific functions and control devices.

Core Subjects

  • Microcontroller programming
  • Electrical engineering and electronics

Practical Skills
